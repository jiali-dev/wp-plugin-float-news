== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I change the style? =
Yes. You can adjust colors, typography, border radius, and animation effects from the settings page or by adding your own CSS.

= Does it support Elementor or Gutenberg? =
Yes. The bar floats independently of page builders and works with any theme or builder.

= Can I limit the number of displayed posts? =
Yes, you can show up to 10 posts at once.

= Can I control where the bar appears? =
Yes. You can choose between **top** or **bottom** position on desktop and mobile.
